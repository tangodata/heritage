About

The aim of this website is to provide up-to-date information of local tango events in Singapore: mainly the regular ones at first but in time will also include the one-off workshops which are guaranteed to take place and not just in planning. Specifically, the “.info” domain was chosen to emphasize the non-profit and community-supported nature of the information you can find here. To that end, the site will be quite spartan apart from a well-maintained calendar and maps showing locations of regular tango venues.

The information presented here is as accurate as what I have been provided with by the various teachers and organisers. However, if in doubt it’s always good to check the Tango Singapore Facebook Group. If you are aware of any inaccuracies on this site, please email me using the Contact Form.
