Tango Doble Frente / double front (tango al reves - inversed / reversed) in which the woman has her back to the man and the man embraces her around the waist, so both dancers face the front/public, it is a very historical style of tango (re)-invented by the popular milongueros such as Antonio Todaro. All steps that one dances in the regular, or "derecho" position, can be danced "al reves".
