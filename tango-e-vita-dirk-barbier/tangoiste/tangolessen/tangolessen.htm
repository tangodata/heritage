Al ritmo del dos por cuatro

The tango rhythm is based on the 2x4, 2 strong beats on 4:
  1   2   3   4
A way to make this 1-2 -mark visible in the act of stepping is ...
on 1 : lifting the knee and the heel of the foot, and slowly starting moving = expanding like a bandoneón.
on 2 : stretching the leg, fast like a clasp-knife, and putting the foot down like a knife-thrust, a thrusting flash, a strong dagger stab to the heart, una puñalada with an Argentinian Facón. A facón is a medium-to-long dagger worn at the back, tucked into the belt of the gaucho in order to be quickly drawn. The facón was the primary tool of the gaucho and the principal piece of combat for the duelo criollo.

  Argentine tango, milonga and tango-waltz are very subtle, sensual private dances, with visual focus on the leg movements. They are examples of irrational or improvising dancing, the style is personal creativity. In tango dancing, the feet of the dancer are not bound to a schematic, pre-designed plan. The dancer is free to decide what he will do on the music he hears.
This is a new orientation of couple dancing. Dance moves or Dance steps, are the building blocks of many dances. More complex dance moves are called dance patterns, dance figures or dance variations. As most dances have a rational-pattern, a figure sequence which can be predicted by the follower, the ballast of previous perceptions about strict rules and ordered sets has to be thrown overboard and replaced by a real communication contact, creating a direct non-verbal dialogue. This requires permanent attention and body connection.
It means that the concept of leading and following is radically different then in predictable dances. The lady follows by "feeling" her partner's chest-changes. Inside the energetic circle of the close dance-hold, she's aware of his movements. Movements must be clear and simple, giving one step-direction at the time, and he should go about it with a quiet and relaxed manner.
Also the act of stepping is philosophy. Physically walking a fluent stream of energy, graceful in shape. The movement power is generated from the lower abdomen and legs. Upper body strength and muscle-tonus can extent the elastic guiding. Guidage de l'homme: There is no pushing or pulling by the man and he also must listen and respond to the way the girl moves.
Women have the abilities and capacities to sabotage, to delay and to dislocate the effect of leading. This gives men a dynamic dimension of uncertainty, which is a good test for their frustration level.
When two people dance together, a dialogue exposes, maybe something of a courtship “dance” rhythm, the bottomline is chemistry.



Tango Salón vs Tango Escenario...

The World Championship Campeonato Mundial de Tango makes a clear difference between the 2 tangos.
The first, the Tango Salón style, is the traditional one of "milongas" classic. The second is the Tango Escenario, also called fantasía. It is tango for stage, it can open doors to the theater, own choreographies, spectacles for tourists or new creations of del arte más viejo del arrabal. It is a tango made spectacle with almost acrobatic skills.
Salon tango is more difficult than a tango fantasy. It is danced to the floor, with much feeling and elegance, without making figures (Es más difícil que un tango fantasía. Se baila al piso, con mucho sentimiento y elegancia, sin hacer figuras) . The pairs can be animated to the barridas, sacadas al piso and enrosques, but saltos, ganchos y piruetas propias del tango de escenario are excluded.

  Tango de Salón Mundial de Baile-guidelines are: an unbroken embrace, style, movement around the floor, closeness to the floor, cadence and rhythm. Once a dancing couple is formed, the partners shall not separate as long as the music is playing. This means that they cannot break the "abrazo", which is considered the Tango dance position. For the position to be considered correct, partners must constantly hold each other by means of embrace. Even though during certain figures it maybe acceptable to disregard this criterion, this should not continue throughout the entire piece. All movements shall be performed within the space allowed by the couples embrace.
A Jury will give special relevance to the couples musicality, elegance and walking style.
Within these guidelines, one may perform any figure commonly used, including barridas, sacadas close to the floor, enrosques. However, ganchos, leaps, trepadas (climbs) and any other typically "Stage Tango" is excluded. Couples, as in a real dancehall, shall constantly move counterclockwise and avoid remaining in the same place for over one or two musical meashures. No dancer may raise his/her legs above the knee line.

The jury watches that the pairs exactly fulfill the basic position (que las parejas cumplan al pie de la letra la posición básica) that characterizes the pasos milongueros of tango salón: el abrazo - the hug - embrace. They watch the position, the presence and the elegance - la postura, la presencia y la elegancia -, everything what the couple does that if one does not listen to the sound, they look from a distant, spot it and say: "they dance tango, as in a postcard" - si uno no escucha el sonido, mira de lejos y decimos `lla bailan tango`, como en una postal.
