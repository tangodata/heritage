Tango al norte

Photos by:
Alex Waterhouse-Hayward

The Tango is a passion from the South, music with Latin roots, a pure essence of Argentina. However, the tango has travelled abroad to spread through the globe the influences that inmigrants from many countries fused in the Rio de la Plata. Alex Waterhouse-Hayward, a Canadian photographer, has captured in his images not only the dance but also the play of seduction so closely related with the Tango.
