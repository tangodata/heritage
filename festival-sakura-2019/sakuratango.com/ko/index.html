사쿠라 탱고 페스티벌 2019
in 후쿠오카
- Abrazo de Asia - 벚꽃, 아시아 그리고 탱고

2019. 3/28 (목) ～ 3/31 (日)

일본의 봄하면 벚꽃을 빼놓을 수 없죠? 벚꽃이 만발한 봄날에 탱고를 통한 문화교류를 목적으로, 일본 후쿠오카의 라틴문화센터 티엠포가 2014년부터 주최하고 있는 규슈 지역 유일한 탱고 국제페스티벌 입니다.
「SAKURA TANGO FESTIVAL in 후쿠오카～ Abrazo de Asia　벚꽃, 아시아 그리고 탱고」
벚꽃 아래에서 탱고의 즐거움을 나누고 만끽할 수 있는 4일간의 여정이 될 것입니다.
6번째를 맞이하는 2019년은 3월 28일(목)부터 3월 31일(일)까지 4일간 개최됩니다! 출연자 등에 관한 최신 정보는 결정되는 대로 수시로 안내하겠습니다.

사브리나 & 루벤 벨리스

프로필

Sabrina & Rubén Veliz

Since 2006, they participated in several tango festivals around the world as teachers and performers.

In 2014 Ruben wrote his book "Secretos del abrazo", about the technique they developed to teach tango. The book is available in spanish, english, russian and italian languages.
In 2015 Ruben was tango stage´s adjudicator in the final round of the Argentinian Tango World Championship.

As choreographers they created "Recuperados" in 2015, "A nuestro hijo" in 2014, "Al sur del sur" for River North Chicago Dance Company in 2011, "Dramatic Tango" in 2010, on tour in Japan, "Tangos Corazonicos" during 2009 in Buenos Aires, and also they created and directed "Tango on stage" on tour in USA.

In 2009 they were juries in Japan, Spain and Chile of the Tango World Championship.

During 2008 they tour China with their show Radio Latina, covering six cities including Shanghai, the same year they tour Japan with the Momoco Aida orchestra and also they present their show "Abrazame" at the Menton Tango Festival in France and they tour Holland with the Amsterdam Sinfonetta presenting their choreography "Last Round".

In july of 2006 within the Soccer World Cup they are featured in a TV Special in Holland.
On April 30, 2005 they made an appearance during the celebration of the 25th anniversary of Queen Beatrix of Holland reign.

Between 2001 to 2005 they perform in different places in Buenos Aires like Esquina Carlos Gardel, El Querandi and Rojo Tango beetween others and also they take part in companies like Tango x 2 and Tango Emocion.

March 1998 through February 2001, they were cast in the Nueva Compañia Tangueros, as first dancers with wich they premiered the show "Milonga Boulevard" in New York, USA, playing the lead couple. Their work received excellent reviews from critic Jack Anderson of the "New York Times".

프로필

MAGDALENA GUTIERREZ & GERMAN BALLEJO


World Champions of Tango 2017

They have developed some traditional dance together with contemporary elements, characterized by their elegance and expressiveness, just like by their original musicality.
Therefore they won the First Place in the Championship Mundial de Tango de Pista 2017.
Throughout their career they have integrated important and renowned companies, performing shows in various theaters in Argentina and in the world.

They have participated in innumerable International Festivals, dictecting courses and shows. As always with the same passion and professionalism which they are characterized.

Magdalena is an internationally recognized dancer, with an enormous elegance and an understanding of Tango as a dance that goes far beyond the dancing as performance or exhibition.
As a dancer, you notice how much you appriciate the hug, the connection and the pleasure on the dance floor, being to underline the strength of her dance and the ability of responding before the requests from the pair, without never losing her identity. She is a creative and musical dancer, with a characteristic and refined footwork, knowing how to put the heart in every movement of her body.
She started her professional career as an artist and a dancer working in various houses of tango porteñas: Complejo Tango, Gala Tangoy Piazzolla Tango, among others.
She integrated the cast of great shows of tango, in the whole world, like: Divino Tango (Compañía Pasiones; Spain, Italy, Greece), Tanguera (Teatro El Nacional, Argentina), Evita (South Korea), Tango Emoción (Teatro El Nacional, Argentina), Chantecler Tango (Paris, Tokyo, Teatro Alvear, Argentina).
She integrates the "Cuadro de Jóvenes de la Academia Nacional del Tango ". In recent years he participated in large international festivals from Europe, America to Asia.

GermánBallejo is a young professional of tango, whoshines for a musical, creative and elegant dance.
With awide story in the scenic stage and his love for the popular ballroom dancing, he is characterized by being adancer as completed, talented acting, expressive, didactic.
He began his learning in tango in 1995 at the age of 10.
His training was given in different academic institutions, andchoreography dance groups. In 2010 he moved to Buenos Aires to start his professional career as an artist and dancerworking in various tango houses as a complex ComplejoTango, Gala Tango and La Ventana, also in companiesas Tempotango and Pasión de Tango going throughnumerous important venues and theaters of Porteños.
In 2014 an accompanyas a dancer the prestigious andfamous Spanish artist, the singer Julio Iglesias. Throughout his career he has taken classes with greatteachers and references such as, Graciela Gonzalez, Gustavo, Naveira and Giselle Anne, Pablo Verón, Rubén and Sabrina,Véliz, Fernando Galera and Vilma Vega, FabiánPeralta,VanesaVillalba and FacundoPiñero, Germán Cornejo, Alejandra Gutti, Luciana Valle, among others.

프로필

Joel Tempestti & Agueda Mansilla

Agueda, a prominent dancer and choreographer from the province of Mendoza, Argentina, with more than 15 years of careers, is recognized by la Honorable Cámara de Senadores from the Province of Mendoza for her contributions of cultural values.
Joel, a professional dancer and teacher of Tango and Argentine folklore, with more than 10 years of experiences and careers, is a member of national recognized casts and prestigious companies.

Both as dance partners since 2008, they worked themselves independently and proudly as members of Seleccionado Fereral de Tango Argentino (in charge of the Ministry of Culture of the Argentine Nation), convened in the beginning of 2016 by a prestigious jury of choreographers and maestros of Tango.

Under the objective of "promoting the culture of Tango and the Argentine Folklore", they have participated in a large range of events in which are stood out: La Fiesta Nacional de la Vendimia, the Bicentennial Festival of the Homeland, Mendoza, EXPOVINIS Y WTM (World Travel Market) Rio de Janeiro - Sau Paulo 2013, Americanto 2014 Festival and 2015, Valparatango Chile 2016, Festival and World Cup Tango 2016 and shows such as AMORES TANGO, TANGOS DE MI VIDA and MARIA DE BUENOS AIRES, Show of the Federal Selection of Tango, among others.

Analia Carreño
Luis Ramirez

아날리아 & 루이스

프로필

Analia Carreño & Luis Ramirez

With 12 years dancing together, this multiple-styled couple have achieved identity and personality in their dance, exquisite and perfect blend of high technique, dynamism, musicality and authentic feeling of the intrinsic roots of Tango Argentino.

After having worked at the most important tango houses and shows in Buenos Aires, South America and Europe, as "Señor Tango", "Esquina Carlos Gardel" and "Tanguera", they choreographed, produced and directed their own tango company "ConjuroTango". With a cast of 26 artists on scene, this great show has sucessfully performed at the Major Theatres and Operas in Argentina and Asia. Apart from that, they have created, directed and performed seven different thematic solo shows, as "Around the world", "The shadow of Malevo"and "All about tango".

Since 2009, they are invited masters to important tango festivals around the world, touring to USA, Australia, Germany, France, Russia, Spain, Japan, China, Korea, Taiwan, Philiphines, Singapore, Malaysia, Brunei and Indonesia. They have come several times to Fukuoka in the last 10 years becoming a reference on these 20 years of tango in our city. Their performances at Sakura Tango have always been breathtaking.

When they are in Buenos Aires, they are invited to perform at the most traditional and popular milongas, as Salon Canning, La Baldosa, Yira Yira and De los Zucca, among others. Moreover, they were invited to dance with prestigious orchestras as Color Tango and Sexteto Milonguero in 2015 and 2016 as well.

In their lessons and workshops, they teach how to dance the sharing the essence of traditional tango with dynamics of nowadays, using natural movements applied to the dance, they have achieved great results in students of all ages around the globe.

La Petit Social del Tango

프로필

La Petit Social del Tango (라 페티트 소시알 델 탱고)

This group is based on traditional arrangements, recalling Orchestras such as Carlos DiSarli, Florindo Sassone, Juan D'arienzo, De Angelis, Troilo among others.
As a special event we have a small set of "chacareras", musical rhythm of the country side with guitar and violin.
Throughout these two years "La Petit Social del Tango" has participated in different festivals and milongas in countries such as Poland, Sweden, Denmark, Spain, Norway, France, Switzerland, among others.
Fortunately he has other great elements within the orchestra, Mauricio Vuoto experienced tango pianist David Merlín virtuoso violinist.
All the members have a long musical experience in tango as in other musical styles.
In short, this Orchestra will leave you wanting to continue dancing all night, as we will experience a beautiful communion between musicians and dancers.

Website: http://clubsocialdeltango.wixsite.com/socialdeltango

Trio Los Fandangos

프로필

Trio Los Fandangos

Naoko Iwatsu (accordion), Taeko Akimoto (Piano), Aogu Tanimoto (violin)

Since its formation in Fukuoka in 1999, the group continues to thoroughly representing the dynamism and fun of Argentine tango. Every spring since 2002, they held the "Tango no Sekku" along with Tango dancers Kenji & Liliana, including performances in Japan. They performed at the “Tokyo Tango Festival 2012”, “Fukuoka Sakura Tango Festival 2014” (since then, they have performed every spring), “Shanghai International Tango Festival”, and “Nara Tango Festival” in 2015. In winter of the same year, they performed for the first time in Europe such as Luxembourg and Vienna and the fourth time in Buenos Aires, Argentina. They have been described as "A sense of air of Buenos Aires, the home of Tango,", "The unthinkable performance intensity of only 3 persons", and "The first band from Japan that makes you dance".

In spring of 2016, they released the new CD “6”.
