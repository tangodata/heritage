Application process

• By all means, please read carefully and apply after the consent.

2. You will receive an e-mail to inform you that Tiempo Iberoamericano has received your application.

3. Those who live in Japan are required to pay the fee within a week either at the reception of Tiempo Iberoamericano or by bank transfer to the designated account. Please note the transfer commission should be at your expense. 
Those living in overseas are required to e-mail a flight reservation copy within a week.

4. You will then receive the confirmation e-mail from Tiempo Iberoamericano to confirm that your application has been accepted.

5. A Voucher for Festival registration will be sent by post mail to those living in Japan and by e-mail attachment to those living overseas.

6. Upon presentation of a print Voucher for Festival Registration at the reception of Tiempo Iberoamericano, a Festival Registration Certificate will be provided.
