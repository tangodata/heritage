Wednesday Tango Night at Le Phare is off to a great start!

The fine autumn weather is here and it just gets everyone in mood to go out again.
After a long wait of nearly three years, the posh bar formerly known as Loft 523 is up and running again.
As you all know we held weekly classes and a milonga there for three years every Tuesday.
Now Le Phare has invited us back to do Wednesday Tango Nights! The place is much the same, only better!
The cool upscale decor and comfy sofas are still there. The great sound system is there. The wood floors are there, with ample space for dancing. Video screens have been added, so we can show tango movies, and selections of dancers in Buenos Aires.
We have a super cute and friendly bartender named Jonathan, who was with us before, and a new manager Bruce (also very cute). Both of them are making us feel welcome, and doing all they can to make Wednesday Tango Nights a long lasting and successful fixture at Le Phare. But the most important element is you!
You made Tuesdays at Loft 523 the most popular milonga in New Orleans, and you are going to make tango at Le Phare fun and happening once again! You know we play all tango all the time, so you can dance from 9 until midnight non stop. The group classes are always fun and informative, so come early and join in.
So see you on Wednesdays, and don't forget our monthly dance at The Country Club on Saturday October 25!
You beautiful people are the best to party with!
