NTSC - DVD

 NOSTALGIA? A tango road movie

On the ocassion of the tenth anniversary of tango legend Astor Piazzolla's death, Dutch bandoneon player Carel Kraayenhof traveled to Buenos Aires with his Sexteto Canyengue. Director Wouter Hasebos follows Carel to an almost bankrupt country that has not good reasons to celebrate. As in a musical road movie, Kraayenhof experiences the nostalgia, homesickness and displacement that's so intrinsic to the raw essence of the tango, while performing at the Teatro Colon with legendary Sexteto Mayor, meeting leading Argentine musicians and paying a poignant visit to an impoverished music school he raised money for in the Netherlands. Length, 55 minutes.

Available in NTSC and PAL DVD format with English subtitles for $20 including shipment within the USA.
